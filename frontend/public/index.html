The Process

How It Works

Three elegant steps from exhaust pipe to ink cartridge.

01

Capture

The X-Carbon device clamps onto a vehicle's exhaust pipe. A precision micro-filter traps PM2.5 soot particles — the finest and most harmful carbon particulates — before they reach the atmosphere.

02

Process

Collected soot is transferred to a micro-processing chamber. It's blended with water, a binding polymer, and a pH stabilizer — creating a stable carbon-black ink suspension with consistent viscosity.

03

Convert

The finished carbon ink is filtered and stored in standardized cartridges compatible with most commercial inkjet and screen-printing systems — ready for use in offices, print shops, or artistic applications.
